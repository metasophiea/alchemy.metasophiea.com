Wavey Boxes

2021-2-19

The world famous, original recipe, EU approved, religiously important, oscar nominated, executive deluxe luxury edition visualisation program that has taken the world by storm and that all the kids are talking about, is here. That's right, Wavey Boxes has made it to desktop.

Made with draft 12 of the project, as a way of testing the inter-process rendering communication, along with the system's ability to perform long-term state-based game-like actions (instead of just rendering a single frame). The wave patterns are calculated on the spot, as are the randomly selected colours.
